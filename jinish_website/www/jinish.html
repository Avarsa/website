About Me

My journey with code began in the early 2010s, a path that has led me through various technical landscapes and philosophical approaches to problem solving. I find myself drawn to projects that require both technical depth and thoughtful consideration of their broader impact.

I believe in systems that reduce complexity rather than add to it. My approach combines careful attention to fundamental principles with a practical focus on creating solutions that truly matter to their users. Lately, I've been exploring the space where automated intelligence meets human-centered design.

I've been fortunate to work extensively with the Frappe Framework, an exceptionally powerful and flexible open source platform. I develop custom applications and enterprise solutions on this framework, with a focus on creating systems that embody simplicity, sustainability, and thoughtful design. My recent work includes creating and maintaining pdadaman.in, the official portal for the Planning & Development Authority of Daman, which streamlines urban planning and development processes.
